About Me

I am Charity Mese, a passionate Business Intelligence and Data Analytics (BIDA) student at Botswana Accountancy College (BAC) in Francistown. I specialize in web and multimedia development, systems development and business intelligence fundamentals.

I consider myself a determined and disciplined learner. Even though I describe myself as a slow learner, I believe in deep understanding rather than rushing concepts. I actively use revision strategies, mock exams, structured study timetables and active recall techniques to improve my academic performance.

My career goal is to become a full stack developer and eventually specialize in building modern, responsive and user focused web applications. I am particularly interested in combining web development with data analytics and artificial intelligence in the future.

Outside academics, I enjoy playing chess and badminton, swimming and going to the gym. These activities help me maintain discipline, strategic thinking and consistency in everything I do.

  • Attention to detail
